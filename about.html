About Sumac Lane

How this blog and company came to be!

After beginning my first year at BYU in 2024, I decided I wanted to continue my education in Experience Design and Management. I felt that creating a blog about all things home, food, family, and design could help women I know who want to amplify their every day living experiences. The simple things we do each day can completely change the way we see life and can help us live more fully!

I've always had a passion for making the mundane fun. I love to help people find beauty, meaning, and fulfillment in the everyday experiences that we so often overlook. This blog is just one way I hope to further accomplish spreading this joy. Sumac Lane was just an idea in passing back in high school, but is now becoming a reality!

So follow along here! We can't wait for you to join us as we celebrate the beauty of our lives and find great purpose and value in making every moment count!
